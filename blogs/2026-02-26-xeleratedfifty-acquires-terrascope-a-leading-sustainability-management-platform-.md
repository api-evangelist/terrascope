Singapore, 27th February, 2026 - XeleratedFifty, a global innovation partner focused on sourcing, scaling and commercialising breakthrough technologies in complex, high-impact industries, today announced it has agreed to acquire Terrascope, the enterprise carbon management and sustainability platform, from Olam Group Limited. Terrascope will continue to operate as a standalone platform under its existing leadership, serving customers across more than 50 countries. The acquisition positions Terrascope for long-term growth under ownership that combines sustainability ambition with proven expertise in scaling innovation and new technologies.
