At Terrascope, our mission is to empower enterprises with the technology and intelligence they need to accelerate their decarbonization journey. As organizations scale their sustainability programs, one challenge consistently stands out: the ability to ingest, validate, and transform emissions-related data coming from multiple systems, formats, and operational processes efficiently and at scale. Today, we are excited to announce Terrascope’s strategic partnership with Citta AI , a modern data engineering and analytics platform designed to bring structure, transparency, and automation to complex data workflows. This collaboration marks a significant leap forward in how Terrascope enables customers to manage sustainability data across their organizations.
