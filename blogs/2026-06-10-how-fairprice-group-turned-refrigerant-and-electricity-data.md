EXECUTIVE SUMMARY Challenge : To meet its operational target of halving Scope 1 and 2 emissions by 2030, FairPrice Group needed to overcome a legacy manual data collection process. Tracking data across hundreds of locations and various business formats was time consuming, inefficient and prone to errors. Solution : Terrascope unified Scope 1 (refrigerants) and Scope 2 (electricity) at the individual store level, combining automated PDF extraction, advanced analytics, and emissions driver analysis.
